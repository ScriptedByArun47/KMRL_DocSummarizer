Here's a summary of the Internshala Resume Guide:

This guide outlines the purpose and essential components of a resume, offering key pointers and common mistakes to avoid for effective self-marketing and securing interview calls.

**Key Points & Actions:**

*   **Resume Purpose:**
    *   A 1-2 page document to market your skills and achievements.
    *   Its primary goal is to secure an interview call.
*   **Resume Content:**
    *   Must include a short summary of qualifications, experience, and other relevant details.
    *   Every piece of information should help the recruiter assess your suitability for the position.
